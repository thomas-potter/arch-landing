What You'll Learn

A structured 6-step path to master environment painting.

1

Design & Thumbnails

Start with strong shapes. Learn to thumbnail 10 ideas in the time it used to take you to render one bad one.

2

Value & Composition

Build readable images in grayscale first. If it doesn't work in black and white, color won't save it.

3

Color Theory

Color harmony, temperature shifts, and atmospheric perspective. Make your worlds feel alive and coherent.

4

Light & Shadow

Use light as a storytelling tool. Dramatic lighting, bounced light, and the mood-altering power of shadow.

5

Material Language

Rock, snow, metal, concrete. each material needs a different approach. Learn when to paint and when to suggest.

Final Workflow

The exact brush setup, layer structure, and shortcuts that speed up your process without killing spontaneity.
